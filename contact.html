Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cleaning services

How do I schedule a cleaning service?

You can schedule a cleaning service through our website by visiting the booking page, calling our customer service number, or sending us an email. We'll confirm your appointment and send you a confirmation with all the details.

What is included in your standard cleaning service?

Our standard cleaning service includes dusting, vacuuming, mopping, bathroom cleaning, kitchen cleaning, and general tidying. We focus on high-touch surfaces and ensure that visible areas are clean and sanitized. For a more detailed cleaning, consider our deep cleaning service.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No, our professional cleaners bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. We use high-quality, eco-friendly products that are effective and safe for your home or office. If you have specific products you'd prefer us to use, please let us know in advance.

How do you screen your cleaning professionals?

All our cleaning professionals undergo thorough background checks, reference verification, and extensive training before joining our team. We only hire reliable, trustworthy individuals who are committed to delivering exceptional cleaning services.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning service?

We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with any aspect of our service, please contact us within 24 hours, and we'll return to address any areas of concern at no additional cost.
